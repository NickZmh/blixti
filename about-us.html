About Us

Blixti is a prepaid digital voucher that makes you more secure when paying online. We will not share your personal details, credit card or bank details, you can pay as easily as cash. However, it is safer than cash, and if you lose the coupon, you can get your money back. You can't say that about cash.

Payment preferences vary widely across the world, reflecting the infinite diversity of historical, technological, cultural, economic and regulatory factors. Local payment methods are now mainstream in some countries, which is why we allow you to use more than 150 local payment methods to recharge coupons, including but not limited to: credit cards, e-wallets, bank transfers and cash methods.

Why Blixty?

Control spending

The temptation to exceed your budget online is great. Blixti puts you back in the driver's seat, helps you control your spending, and sets personal limits. Blixti do not support recurring payments as per default – therefore no additional money will be charged.

Private and Secure

We don't need any of your personal details. We do not share any sensitive customers data to any other related parties.

No credit card required

Pay online without a bank account or credit card. Blixti Digital Voucher eliminates step of using credit card on merchants website.

Purchase globally, pay locally

Buy Blixti Digital Voucher instantly using your local payment methods. Over 150 payment methods in 200 countries available.
